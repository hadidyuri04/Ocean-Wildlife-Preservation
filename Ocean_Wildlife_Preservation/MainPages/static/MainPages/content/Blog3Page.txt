The Coral Resiliency Project: Engineering Survival
Scientific Innovation
Assisted evolution and coral restoration.
As ocean temperatures rise, traditional conservation isn't enough. Scientists are now exploring "assisted evolution"—breeding corals that have a naturally higher tolerance for heat. By out-planting these resilient "super-corals" onto dying reefs, researchers hope to provide a biological bridge that allows reef ecosystems to survive while the world works to reduce carbon emissions.
