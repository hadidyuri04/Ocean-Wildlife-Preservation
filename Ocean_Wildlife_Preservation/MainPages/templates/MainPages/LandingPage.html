About

Ocean wildlife preservation

is the effort to protect and restore marine ecosystems and the species that depend on them. Oceans are home to an incredible diversity of life, from tiny plankton to massive whales, and they play a vital role in maintaining Earth’s balance by producing oxygen, regulating climate, and supporting global food chains. However, marine life is increasingly threatened by pollution, overfishing, habitat destruction, and climate change. Conservation efforts focus on reducing plastic waste, protecting endangered species, establishing marine protected areas, and promoting sustainable fishing practices. By preserving ocean wildlife, we help ensure healthier oceans for future generations and maintain the stability of the planet’s natural systems.
